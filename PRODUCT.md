# Dashboard Profesor de Matemáticas

## Product Purpose
Tool for a mathematics teacher to track students, their subjects, schools, and monthly fees. Used between classes and at end of month for billing.

## Register
product

## Users
A single math teacher in Mexico who teaches multiple students across different schools (secondary, preparatory, university level). Uses the dashboard on a laptop during the day.

## Tone
Professional but warm. Organized and clear. No corporate coldness — this is a personal tool.

## Brand
- Primary: deep blue-indigo (educational, trustworthy)
- Accent: emerald green (for money/cobros)
- Neutral backgrounds with subtle blue tint

## Anti-references
- No dark mode (teacher uses it in daytime)
- No excessive decoration
- No dashboard overload — keep it scannable
